## Built‑in permissions
1. **Admin**  
   - Can perform all actions  
   - Can impersonate another user  

1. **Edit project naming wizard**  
   - Full access to modify naming wizard configuration  

1. **View project naming wizard**  
   - Read‑only access to naming wizard configuration  

1. **Full access to record/edit/publish videos**  
   - May create new video projects  
   - May record videos  
   - May edit videos  
   - May publish videos  

1. **May view reports at their level or below**  
   - Level 3 users can view reports for Levels 0–3  
   - This allows managers to see reports across subordinate levels
