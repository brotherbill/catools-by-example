# User Hierarchy
###### C:/dev/catools-by-example/03-user-hierarchy/1-user-hierarchy.md

## The company may define their hierarchical structure, with their own names
1. Level 0: Individual user  
1. Level 1: Team name (or equivalent)  
1. Level 2: Department name  
1. Level 3: Division name  
1. Level 4: Company name  

Up to **10 levels** may be defined.  
That is sufficient for all known organizational structures.
